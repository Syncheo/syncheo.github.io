Syncheo supports organizations in fully mastering their **IBM Engineering Lifecycle Management (ELM)** environment. Whether you are deploying, upgrading, or optimizing, our services cover the entire lifecycle of your platform.

---

## IBM ELM Expertise {#expertise}

We act as a trusted technical partner across the IBM Jazz ELM ecosystem, including **DOORS Next (DNG)**, **Engineering Workflow Management (EWM)** and **Engineering Test Management (ETM)**.

Our strategic consulting helps you define the target architecture, optimize your development processes, and ensure your environment complies with industry standards (DO-178C, ISO 26262, EN 50128…).

---

## Custom Development {#developpement}

IBM ELM tools offer powerful extension points that we leverage to adapt the platform to your specific business processes.

- **OpenSocial Widgets for DNG**: automating requirements checks and injecting business logic directly into the DOORS Next interface.
- **Dojo Plugins for EWM**: deep customization of the web interface, conditional attribute display, custom visual workflows.
- **Server-side Extensions (Java SDK)**: development of advisors and participants to automate delivery and validation rules in EWM.
- **OSLC Integration**: interconnecting lifecycle tools (DNG, EWM, ETM, Jira, GitGuardian, etc.).

---

## Administration & Infrastructure {#administration}

A high-performing ELM environment relies on a well-sized and properly configured infrastructure.

- Installation and configuration of complex topologies (JTS, LQE, GC, data servers).
- Upgrades to **IBM ELM 7.x** with data migration and validation.
- Performance optimization (LQE queries, indexing, JVM tuning).
- Implementation of backup strategies and disaster recovery plans.
